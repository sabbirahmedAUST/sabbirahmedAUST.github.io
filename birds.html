Cockatiels are native to Australia.
They’re small parrots with big personalities.
They learn routines fast and love attention.
Their crest shows mood and energy.
Crest up = alert/curious.
Crest flat = relaxed or resting.
Crest pinned back can mean stress.
Cockatiels communicate constantly.
Whistles and chirps mean different things.
They also “talk” with posture and tail movement.
Diet matters more than people think.
Seeds only can cause health issues over time.
Pellets + veggies + clean water is better daily.
They bond strongly with their humans.
Boredom can lead to screaming or feather issues.
Toys, foraging, and time together help a lot.
